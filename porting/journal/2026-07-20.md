## Split vector plan construction by responsibility (R5.3)

Commit date: 2026-07-20
Source commit: this commit

`vector/plan/mod.rs` (1,403 production lines) becomes three files behind an
unchanged facade:

- `build.rs` (1,027) — `build_vector_plan(_with_lockstep)`, `PlacementState`,
  ordinary dependency edges, effect-conflict orientation, and the producer's
  type/rate conversions;
- `fusion.rs` (556) — fused serial-group construction (sample closure,
  dangerous delayed-read relations, conflicting effect users);
- `producer_reachability.rs` (104) — `PlanReachability` bitset reachability,
  header-documented as deliberately separate from the checker's
  `verify::checker_reachability` (trust boundary, plan §4.6);
- `mod.rs` (127) — facade, `VerifiedVectorPlan` wrapper, build-error
  taxonomy, and the `#[cfg(test)]` `verified_vector_plan_for_test` helper.

Move only: bodies unchanged apart from `pub(super)` on sibling-crossed
items and per-file imports. With R5.1 (analysis) and R5.2 (verify), phase R5
is complete: producer and checker reachability now live in visibly disjoint
modules on both sides of the plan artifact. Validation: transform 385,
`vector_mode` oracle 35/35, clippy `-D warnings` clean, fmt applied.
