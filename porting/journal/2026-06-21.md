## Extract BraState from SignalToFirLower (W9 pilot)

Commit date: 2026-06-21
Source commit: this commit

First commit of the `SignalToFirLower` god-struct decomposition plan
(`signal-to-fir-lower-struct-decomposition-plan-2026-06-20-en.md`, cluster 1 of 7).

Extracted the five BRA-specific fields from `SignalToFirLower` into a new
`BraState` sub-struct defined in `bra.rs`:

- `bra_state_scheduled` → `bra.scheduled`
- `bra_grad_cache` → `bra.grad_cache`
- `bra_delay1_carry_vars` → `bra.delay1_carry_vars`
- `bra_delay_array_carry_vars` → `bra.delay_array_carry_vars`
- `bra_tape_store_var` → `bra.tape_store_var`

`BraState` derives `Default`; `setup.rs::new` now initialises with
`bra: BraState::default()` in place of the five individual `HashMap::new()` /
`HashSet::new()` calls.  All original field doc comments were moved verbatim into
the struct definition.
